Dominion massacre leaves 31 dead

By Jake Sisko, stardate 51825.6

Deep Space Nine - A tragic event took the lives of 31 young Red Squad cadets in dominion space. I was myself involved in the event, which was fueled by their delusional captain that attempted a suicide mission. Their mission was to take down a massive Galaxy Class Dominion starship with a risky plan that was bound to fail. The crewmembers, some as young as 17, were so devoted to complete their mission and defend the honor of Starfleet’s Red Squad that I was locked up in the brig after attempting to convince them that their mission would fail horribly.

After spending a few days with the crew it became increasingly apparent that their captain was manipulative and dictatorial. No crewmember was allowed to remember home, or even speak of it for that matter. Their intrapersonal relationships were limited only to the dominion war and a blind devotion to Starfleet and honor. The captain’s manipulation was so effective that my Ferenghi friend, Nog, was lured to accept a chief engineering role that was key to the planning of the mission. Nog helped them increase the warp drive’s capabilities which allowed them to get to the dominion ship.
